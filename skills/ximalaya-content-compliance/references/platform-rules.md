# Ximalaya Platform Rules — Complete Reference

> Sourced from official Ximalaya announcements and help center documentation, verified 2026-06-08.
> This document serves as the legal and operational basis for content compliance checks.

## 1. Platform Regulatory Framework

### 1.1 Core Principles
1. Comply with national laws and regulations
2. Do not produce illegal or rule-violating content
3. Respect user rights and platform community standards

### 1.2 Governing Laws
- Cybersecurity Law of the PRC
- Internet Information Service Management Regulations
- Internet Religious Information Service Management Measures (effective 2022-03-01)
- Network Anchor Behavior Standards (issued by NRTA and MCT, June 2022)

## 2. Domain-Specific Qualification Requirements

| Domain | Required Qualifications | Without Qualifications |
|--------|------------------------|------------------------|
| **News (新闻)** | Internet News Information Service Permit (互联网新闻信息服务许可) | Cannot publish political, economic, military, diplomatic news or commentary, or social incident reports |
| **Health (健康)** | Medical practitioner license, public nutritionist certificate, psychological counselor certificate, or medical institution practice permit | Cannot publish disease treatment guidance, medication advice, or other professional health content |
| **Finance (财经)** | Securities/fund/futures/insurance practice license, investment analyst certificate, financial planner certificate, or journalist/press card | Cannot publish stock trading guidance, investment advice, or other professional financial content |
| **Religion (宗教)** | N/A — **completely prohibited** | All religious content is barred from listing |
| **Legal (法律)** | Legal professional qualification | Cannot publish legal advice content |
| **Campus Radio** | School-issued authorization with official seal | Cannot operate as campus radio |

**Permanent Rejection:** Repeatedly submitting health/finance content applications without uploading qualifications results in permanent rejection.

## 3. Absolutely Prohibited Content (A-Class Violations)

These result in immediate content removal + live-stream ban (7 days for first offense, permanent for severe cases):

1. Content opposing the fundamental principles established by the Constitution
2. Content endangering national unity, sovereignty, and territorial integrity
3. Content leaking state secrets, endangering national security, or harming national honor and interests
4. Content inciting ethnic hatred, ethnic discrimination, or undermining ethnic unity
5. Content promoting cults or superstition
6. Content disrupting social order or undermining social stability
7. Content inducing minors to commit crimes or depicting violence, pornography, gambling, or terrorism
8. Content insulting or defaming others, violating personal privacy or other legitimate rights
9. Content endangering social morality or damaging ethnic cultural traditions
10. Other content prohibited by laws, administrative regulations, and rules

## 4. Religious Content — Zero Tolerance

Per the **Internet Religious Information Service Management Measures** (effective 2022-03-01):

- ❌ No online missionary work
- ❌ No religious education or training
- ❌ No publishing or relaying scripture lectures/sermons
- ❌ No organizing religious activities online
- ❌ No live broadcasting or recording of religious ceremonies
- ❌ No fundraising in the name of religion
- ❌ No content inciting religious fanaticism or undermining national religious policies
- ❌ No superstitious content: bazi (八字), fengshui (风水), fortune-telling (算命)

## 5. News Content — Qualification-Gated

**Definition of "News Information":** Political news information, including reports and commentary on politics, economy, military, diplomacy, and other public affairs, as well as reports and commentary on social emergencies.

**Specific prohibitions without qualification:**
- Publishing or relaying user-generated political audio/video programs
- Content containing foreign media or radio station material
- Any content tagged as "news" or using news-style presentation

## 6. Finance Content — Qualification-Gated

**Prohibited without qualification:**
- Stock/security recommendations (荐股)
- Gambling/lottery recommendations (荐彩)
- Investment guidance or advice
- P2P lending promotion
- Blockchain/cryptocurrency investment recommendations
- Various "online money-making" schemes

## 7. Health Content — Qualification-Gated

**Prohibited without qualification:**
- Disease diagnosis or treatment guidance
- Medication recommendations or usage instructions
- Promotion of non-regular medical facilities or services
- Promotion of illegal medical practices (surrogacy, fetal gender testing, illegal organ transplantation)
- Non-professional TCM treatment guidance (acupuncture, moxibustion, scraping, cupping, acupressure)
- Exaggerated treatment claims (e.g., "100% effective")
- Health scare-mongering not based on scientific facts

## 8. Other Important Restrictions

### 8.1 Small Language Content
Audiovisual programs or live streams in the following languages are **NOT allowed** for listing:
German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Ukrainian, Swedish, Czech, Albanian, Polish, Serbian, Persian, Thai, Korean, Vietnamese, Arabic, Japanese, Hindi, Malay, Burmese, Sinhala, Hausa, Swahili, etc.

### 8.2 Content Quality Standards (effective 2025-08-18)
- Albums require cover images >30-word descriptions
- Audio quality: no noise, popping, distortion, or excessive background
- Mandarin pronunciation must be standard (dialect content exempted)
- AI-generated audio must not sound excessively mechanical
- Album structure must have clear chapter ordering

### 8.3 Malicious Marketing
Content containing any of the following is prohibited:
- Contact information (phone, WeChat, email, QR codes)
- Marketing links or off-platform redirects
- Private/offline transaction solicitation
- Pure advertising content
- Reselling paid premium programs

### 8.4 Map Standards
- China maps must be complete and accurate
- No omitted or altered place names
- Correct administrative region designations required

### 8.5 Title Quality
Titles must not be:
- Exaggerated, clickbait, or coercive
- Mismatched with actual content
- Containing typos, grammatical errors, or punctuation abuse

## 9. Violation Penalty System

**Audio Content:**
1. Content removal
2. Temporary posting restriction
3. Permanent account suspension

**Live Streaming (escalating):**
1. Remove violating content → Deduct penalty fees → Cancel live permissions (1-7 days)
2. Recover illegal gains → Permanently cancel live permissions → Permanently close account
3. Restrict store permissions/product management → Clear store functionality

**Appeal Window:** 7 days from violation notification
